power supply

The power supply is the part of the computer that supplies power. It's job is to take in big power like 120vAC-US or 240vAC-EU, or in simpler terms instant death, and turn it into +3.3vDC, +5vDC, and +12vDC. It first reduces the voltage with a transformer, it then turns it from AC alternating current to DC direct current in a rectifier. However the out put is not smooth so it goes through a filter to get out a constant stream of power. And the final step is useing voltage regulators to get the desired 3.3/5/12 volts.
