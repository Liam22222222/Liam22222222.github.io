motherboard

The motherboard is usually referred to as the heart of the computer. This is because the motherboard houses, connects, and delivers power to all the components in the computer. It is also in charge of housing the Bios. The Bios tells all the components what other components are, where they are and how to use them. It's also what lets you flash an OS so the Bios is only kind of important.


Three types of motherboards:
