motherboard

The motherboard is usally referred to as the heart of the computer. This is because the motherboard houses, connects, and delivers power to all the componets in the commputer. It is also incharged of houseing the Bios. The Bios tells all the componets what other componets are, where they are and how to use them. It's also what lets you flash an OS so the Bios is only kind of important.
