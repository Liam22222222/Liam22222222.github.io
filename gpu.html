graphics processing unit

Despite the name, the GPU dosen't just handle graphics. While the CPU is good at doing things over and over again in a sequence, which is good for reading and running instrucions and programs. The GPU is good at doing alot of things all at once, like drawing 4 million pixiles 120 times every second (FPS: frames per second). But it's also good for things like simulations or traing AI because you need to do many tasks all at once. You may be wondering why we can't just have the cpu multi task and it's because in order to achive this GPUs are massive, power hungry, loud, and exspinsive. For reference a decent CPU is about $260 while a high end GPU is over $2,000! And pulls over 60 watts; 20volts 3amps, yikes. But you can't ignore the binifits becaues even with a low end gpu you can get a at least 40% performance increase!
